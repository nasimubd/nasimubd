MARTIAL ARTS

We offer various Martial Arts sessions to suit your training needs and budget. In our Basic membership you will have twice-weekly classes in one martial art. For more frequent training, intermediate and advanced options are available. These include three or five sessions per week, with the advanced option allowing you to train in two different martial arts. If you crave unlimited access, elite membership would be a perfect choice. Finally, junior membership holders can access all the children's martial arts classes.
