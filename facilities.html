A LARGE GYM

The colossal gym pulsed with energy. Sweat glistened on bodies moving between a labyrinth of weight machines. In one corner, a group grunted during a high-intensity interval training class. Another area offered rows of treadmills with runners gazing out at cityscape views. A rock-climbing wall challenged enthusiasts, while a separate room provided a tranquil space for yogis.
